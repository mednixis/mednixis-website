Privacy Policy

How Mednixis collects, uses, and protects the information you share with us.

Information We Collect

When you contact Mednixis through this website, we collect the information you provide in our contact form, such as your name, position, organization, location, contact details, and the details of your inquiry.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide solely to review your inquiry, assess your organization’s needs, respond to your request, and communicate with you about our services. We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties.

Confidentiality

Confidentiality is a core value at Mednixis. All inquiries and client communications are treated with strict discretion. Information about your organization, project, or strategy is never disclosed publicly without your consent.

We take reasonable measures to protect the information you share with us against unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.
